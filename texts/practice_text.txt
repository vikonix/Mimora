The small town wakes up early every morning.
The bakery opens its doors and smells of freshly baked bread.
Children walk to school while the birds sing in the trees.
In the square, people greet each other and talk about the weather.
In the afternoon, many sit on a cafe terrace to rest.
When night falls, the streets fill with music and laughter.
