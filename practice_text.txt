Hello and welcome to EchoLoop, your friendly pronunciation trainer.

Here is how it works. The app shows you a short phrase and reads it aloud. You repeat the phrase out loud, and EchoLoop compares your voice with the example. 
Then it shows a score and points out the words you can improve.

You can edit this text freely. The practice phrases are built from whatever you write here, so feel free to paste your own paragraph, a song, or a few sentences you want to master.

Take your time, speak clearly, and have fun learning English.
